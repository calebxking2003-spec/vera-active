Returns & Refund Policy

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery (delivery date is based on carrier tracking).

Damaged/defective on arrival: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery and include clear photo or video proof of the issue and packaging. We’ll review and confirm next steps.

Refunds are issued after the return is received and inspected. Shipping costs are non‑refundable.
