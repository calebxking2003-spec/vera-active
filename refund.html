Returns & Refund Policy

We want you to be satisfied with your purchase.

Returns are accepted within 14 days of delivery.

To be eligible for a return:

  • The item must be unused and in original condition
  • The product must be returned in its original packaging
  • The original box must not be damaged

If your item arrives damaged or defective, you must contact us within 48 hours of delivery and provide clear photo or video proof of the issue.

Refunds are issued once the returned item is received and inspected. Shipping costs are non‑refundable.
